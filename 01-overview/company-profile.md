## Flagship Products

### FiNSTA — NBFC Lending Management Platform
Primary revenue driver and the focus of this CTO assessment.

**Capabilities:**
- Mobile and web-based fintech platform
- Full loan lifecycle: customer onboarding, e-KYC, credit assessment, multi-stage loan approvals, disbursement, repayment, accounting, auditing, RBI compliance reporting
- Loan configurator: products configured at varying interest rates by borrower profile
- Scheme configurator: festive/occasion-based special loan schemes
- Centralised borrower verification database
- Real-time creditworthiness assessment
- Automated loan documentation generation

**Segments served:**
| Segment | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| NBFCs | Non-Banking Finance Companies (RBI registered) |
| Nidhi companies | Mutual benefit finance companies |
| MACS | Mutually Aided Credit Cooperative Societies |
| RRBs | Regional Rural Banks |
| PACS | Primary Agricultural Credit Societies |
| Small cooperative banks | State-licensed cooperative lending institutions |

### EasyCHIT — Chit Fund Management Software
Comprehensive chit fund ERP covering operations, scheme management, member portfolio tracking, and revenue reporting. Serves Kapil Chits (captive) and external chit fund operators.

### Hexabuild — Construction ERP
Procurement, inventory, project progress tracking, BOQ management, accounting, and reporting for real estate and construction firms.
